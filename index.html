Introduction

I am a Data Science Faculty Fellow & Courant Instructor at NYU Center for Data Science & Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences. I graduated from MIT EECS in June 2023 under the supervision of Yury Polyanskiy. I was a member at IAS School of Mathematics from Sept. 2023 to July 2023.

My research interests include high-dimensional statistics, information theory, theoretical computer science, and probability.
